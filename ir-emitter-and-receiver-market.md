<p><p>The IR emitter and receiver market can be divided into different segments based on the wavelength range they operate in. Short wavelength infrared (SWIR) devices function in the range of 0.9 to 1.7 microns, medium wavelength infrared (MWIR) devices operate in the range of 3 to 5 microns, while long wavelength infrared (LWIR) devices operate in the range of 8 to 14 microns. Far infrared (FIR) devices have a longer wavelength above 15 microns. Each market segment offers unique applications and use cases based on their wavelength characteristics.</p></p>
