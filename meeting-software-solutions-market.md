<p><p>The meeting software solutions market can be divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers, enabling users to access the software and all its features through an internet connection. They offer flexibility, scalability, and ease of use. On the other hand, on-premise solutions require installation on individual computers or servers within an organization's network. This type provides more control over data security and customization options but may involve higher upfront costs and maintenance responsibilities.</p></p>
