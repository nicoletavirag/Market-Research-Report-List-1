<p><p>Food safety testing refers to the process of examining food products to ensure that they are safe for consumption and free from any contaminants that may pose a risk to human health. These technologies include traditional methods like culture-based testing and rapid methods like PCR and immunoassay-based tests. They are used by food producers, regulatory authorities, and third-party laboratories to ensure the safety and quality of food products.</p><p>The future of the food safety testing and technologies market looks promising. The increasing awareness of foodborne diseases and the growing focus on food safety regulations are driving the demand for food safety testing.
