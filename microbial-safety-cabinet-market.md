<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Microbial Safety Cabinet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Level 1</li><li>Level 2</li><li>Level 3</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microbial safety cabinets are categorized into three levels based on their containment capabilities. Level 1 cabinets are suitable for low to moderate risk applications and provide protection to the user and the environment. Level 2 cabinets offer increased protection by incorporating HEPA filters that prevent the release of harmful aerosols. They are used for moderate-risk applications involving biological agents. Level 3 cabinets provide the highest level of containment, with the ability to handle high-risk pathogens, as they feature additional features like double seals and negative pressure systems to prevent any accidental release of contaminants.</p></p>
