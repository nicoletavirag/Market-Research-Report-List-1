<p><p>The Box Spring Mattress market offers various types of mattresses, including Linked Spring, One-Line Rigid Spring, Independent Canister Spring, and Others. Linked Spring mattresses are constructed with interconnected springs, providing consistent support. One-Line Rigid Spring mattresses have springs arranged in a single line, offering firmness and stability. Independent Canister Spring mattresses contain individual pocketed springs, providing personalized support and reducing motion transfer. The "Others" category includes mattresses with unique features or combinations of materials not classified in the above categories. These different types cater to diverse sleeping preferences and comfort needs of consumers.</p></p>
