<p><p>VSAT antennas can be classified into two main types: fixed and portable. Fixed VSAT antennas are permanently installed in a specific location and are typically used for stable and continuous communication. They are commonly employed in situations where a consistent and reliable connection is required, such as in remote offices or rural areas. On the other hand, portable VSAT antennas are designed for easy transportation and rapid deployment. They offer flexibility and mobility, making them suitable for temporary setups, disaster response, military applications, or any scenario that demands portable connectivity solutions.</p></p>
