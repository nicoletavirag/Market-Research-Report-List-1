<p><p>Rapid Liquid Printing (RLP) is an innovative 3D printing technology that enables the creation of large-scale, complex objects in a matter of minutes. The material solidifies rapidly upon contact with the gel, allowing for the creation of intricate and functional objects.</p><p>The market for RLP has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue expanding at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 10.90% during the forecast period.
