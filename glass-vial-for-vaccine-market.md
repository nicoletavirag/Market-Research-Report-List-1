<p><p>The Glass Vial for Vaccine market consists of two main types: Molded Glass Vial and Tubular Glass Vial. Molded Glass Vials are made by melting glass and pouring it into a mold, resulting in a consistent shape and size. They are commonly used for vaccines that require high precision dosage. Tubular Glass Vials, on the other hand, are manufactured by drawing glass tubes and then forming them into vials. They are more suitable for vaccines that need larger volumes and can withstand higher pressure. Both types play a crucial role in safely storing and transporting vaccines.</p></p>
