<p><p>A dental floss stick, also known as a floss pick, is a small, disposable dental tool used to floss teeth. Dental floss sticks are widely used as an alternative to regular dental floss due to their ease of use and portability.</p><p>The dental floss stick market has been experiencing steady growth and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of dental diseases such as gum disease and cavities has further boosted the market growth.</p><p>Additionally, the convenience and user-friendly nature of dental floss sticks have made them popular among consumers who find traditional flossing methods challenging or time-consuming. <p><p>The dental floss stick market can be classified into two types: round line and flat line.
