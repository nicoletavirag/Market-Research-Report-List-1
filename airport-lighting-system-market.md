<p><p>The Airport Lighting System Market is segmented into various types. Approach lights are used to guide pilots during the landing phase. Runway lights are positioned along the runway edge and centerline, providing visual guidance for takeoff and landing. Taxiway and apron lights help aircraft navigate around the airport, especially during taxiing. Stop bars are illuminated lights used at runway intersections to instruct aircraft to stop or proceed. Other types include PAPI lights for positioning during approach, REIL lights for runway end identification, and wind direction-indicating lights.</p></p>
