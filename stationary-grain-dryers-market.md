<p><p>Stationary grain dryers are essential equipment used for drying grains to prevent spoilage and ensure optimal storage conditions. There are two main types of stationary grain dryers in the market: horizontal dryers and vertical dryers. Horizontal dryers typically consist of a rotating drum that tumbles the grains to evenly distribute heat and moisture for efficient drying. Vertical dryers, on the other hand, utilize a tall, cylindrical design to achieve the same drying process. Both types of dryers are commonly used in agricultural settings to preserve and protect grain harvests.</p></p>
