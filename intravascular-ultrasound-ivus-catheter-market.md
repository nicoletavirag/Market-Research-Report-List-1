<p><p>An Intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) catheter is a medical device used in the field of interventional cardiology. It is a thin, flexible tube that is inserted into the blood vessels to capture high-resolution images of the inside of the arteries. IVUS catheters use advanced imaging technology, such as sound waves, to provide cross-sectional images of blood vessels, allowing doctors to assess their structure, wall thickness, and any abnormality or blockages.</p><p>The global Intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) catheter market is witnessing steady growth and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 5.3% during the forecast period. The market growth is primarily driven by factors such as the rising incidence of cardiovascular diseases and the increasing demand for minimally invasive procedures. IVUS catheters provide precise and accurate visualization of blood vessels, enabling doctors to make better decisions during interventions. The growing adoption of these devices among healthcare professionals is fueling market growth.</p><p>Several trends are shaping the Intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) catheter market. Firstly, there is a growing preference for the use of IVUS catheters in complex procedures like coronary interventions and bifurcation lesions. The ability of IVUS to provide detailed information about vessel characteristics and stent sizing has made it a valuable tool in such cases. Secondly, technological advancements are driving the development of innovative IVUS catheters that offer improved imaging quality and enhanced performance. These advancements are expected to widen the market adoption of IVUS catheters.</p><p>In conclusion, the Intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) catheter market is witnessing steady growth, propelled by factors like the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases and the demand for minimally invasive procedures.
