<p><p>The sandwich panel press market can be classified into two types: discontinuous and continuous. In the discontinuous market, the press is operated manually, with each panel being pressed individually. This type is suitable for small-scale operations that require flexibility and different panel sizes. On the other hand, the continuous market involves the use of automated machinery that presses multiple panels simultaneously. This type is ideal for large-scale production where high volumes of panels need to be manufactured quickly and efficiently.</p></p>
