They are easy to install and are ideal for smaller spaces. On the other hand, passive subwoofers require an external amplifier to provide power and enhance bass production. They are more customizable but may require additional installation steps. Both types of subwoofers enhance the audio experience in cars by emphasizing low-frequency sounds and delivering deeper and more robust bass.</p></p>
