<p><ul><li>Power Off Brake</li><li>Power On Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electromagnetic brakes market consists of two main types: power off brakes and power on brakes. Power off brakes are designed to engage when power is disconnected, providing a fail-safe mechanism in case of power failure. This type is commonly used in elevators, escalators, and safety applications. Power on brakes, on the other hand, are energized when power is applied, allowing them to provide continuous braking force. This type is widely used in vehicles, industrial machinery, and automation systems where precise control of braking is required.</p></p>
