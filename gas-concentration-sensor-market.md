<p><p>Gas concentration sensors are devices used to measure the concentration of gases in a given environment. Two common types of gas concentration sensors are zirconia sensors and titanium dioxide oxygen sensors. Zirconia sensors utilize a ceramic material called zirconium dioxide, which can measure the concentration of oxygen in gases. Titanium dioxide oxygen sensors, on the other hand, use titanium dioxide as the sensing material. Both sensors are widely used in various industries such as automotive, environmental monitoring, and healthcare to ensure accurate gas concentration measurements.</p></p>
