<p><p>The street and roadway lighting market can be classified into two types: conventional lighting and smart lighting. Conventional lighting refers to traditional lighting systems that use high-pressure sodium, metal halide, or mercury vapor lamps. These systems are typically controlled manually and consume more energy. On the other hand, smart lighting utilizes advanced technologies such as sensors, wireless communication, and data analytics to optimize energy usage and improve functionality. Smart lighting systems can automatically adjust brightness levels, detect motion, and monitor lighting performance, making them more energy-efficient and cost-effective.</p></p>
