<p><p>Natural sweeteners are categorized into two types based on their sweetness intensity: high intensity and low intensity sweeteners. High intensity sweeteners are much sweeter than table sugar and are used in very small quantities to achieve the desired level of sweetness. Examples of high intensity sweeteners include stevia, monk fruit, and aspartame. On the other hand, low intensity sweeteners are less sweet than table sugar and are used in larger quantities to provide sweetness. Examples of low intensity sweeteners include honey, agave nectar, and maple syrup. These natural sweeteners cater to different preferences and dietary needs in the market.</p></p>
