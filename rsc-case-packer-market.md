<p><p>The RSC Case Packer Market is divided into two main types: Semi-automatic Packers and Fully Automatic Packers. Semi-automatic Packers require human intervention to load the cases into the machine and do not possess automatic case forming capabilities. These packers are often suitable for smaller production lines where manual labor is available. On the other hand, Fully Automatic Packers can independently form, load, and seal cases without any human involvement. They are preferred in larger production facilities with high-speed requirements and minimal labor involvement.</p></p>
