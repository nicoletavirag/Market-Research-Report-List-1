<p><p>Passive UHF Inlay market types include UHF Dry Inlay and UHF Wet Inlay. UHF Dry Inlay is a type of passive RFID inlay that is typically pre-cut and delivered without adhesive backing. It is designed to be adhered to a surface using an external adhesive, allowing flexibility in application. On the other hand, UHF Wet Inlay comes with an adhesive backing and is ready to be directly applied to objects or surfaces. This makes it a convenient option for quick and easy installation. Both types provide reliable and efficient identification and tracking capabilities.</p></p>
