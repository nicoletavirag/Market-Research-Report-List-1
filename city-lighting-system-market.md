High Pole Street Lights are tall structures designed to provide illumination over large areas such as highways or big-scale public spaces. Medium Post Lights are moderately tall and are commonly used for lighting residential streets or smaller public areas. Low Post Lights are shorter and mostly used for illuminating walkways, parks, or small gardens. These different types of lighting systems cater to diverse lighting requirements within a city environment.</p></p>
