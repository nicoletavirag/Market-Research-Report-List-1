These systems utilize smart sensors that monitor soil moisture levels, weather conditions, and other environmental factors to optimize water usage. This trend is driven by the need for water conservation and increased efficiency in irrigation practices.</p><p>Another emerging trend in the market is the integration of wireless connectivity and smartphone applications into buried sprinkler systems. <p><p>The buried sprinkler market can be categorized into two main types: adjustable and non-adjustable sprinklers. Adjustable sprinklers are designed with features that allow users to manually adjust the spray pattern, reach, and intensity of water. This flexibility enables users to customize the watering range according to their specific needs. On the other hand, non-adjustable sprinklers have a fixed spray pattern and reach, providing a consistent and reliable watering performance without the need for manual adjustments. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements in terms of control and convenience in the irrigation process.</p></p>
