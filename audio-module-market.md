<p><p>An audio module is an electronic device that is used to add sound or audio functionality to various products such as smartphones, tablets, computers, home appliances, and automotive systems. Audio modules are highly customizable and can be tailored to specific requirements, enabling manufacturers to integrate high-quality audio capabilities into their products easily.</p><p>The audio module market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. One of the major drivers of this growth is the increasing demand for smart devices with advanced audio functionalities.
