<p><p>The social intranet software market consists of two main types: cloud and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based social intranet software refers to applications that are hosted and operated on remote servers by a third-party provider. It offers the flexibility of accessing the software and data from anywhere with an internet connection. On the other hand, on-premises social intranet software is installed and maintained on a company's own servers, providing more control and security but requiring internal resources to manage and support the software.</p></p>
