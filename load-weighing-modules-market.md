<p><p>Load weighing modules are devices used to measure the weight of objects. Static load weighing modules are designed for use in stationary or fixed applications, such as weighing tanks or silos. They provide accurate weight measurements for static loads. Dynamic load weighing modules, on the other hand, are designed for use in moving or dynamic applications, such as weighing conveyor belts or forklifts. These modules can measure weight accurately even when the load is in motion, allowing for real-time weight monitoring and control.</p></p>
