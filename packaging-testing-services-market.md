<p><p>Packaging testing services encompass three main market types: physical testing, chemical testing, and microbiological testing. Physical testing evaluates the physical attributes of packaging materials, such as strength, durability, and flexibility. Chemical testing determines the chemical composition of packaging materials to ensure they meet regulatory standards and do not contain harmful substances. Microbiological testing checks for the presence of microorganisms in packaging to prevent contamination and maintain product integrity. These testing services are crucial for ensuring the safety, quality, and compliance of packaging materials in various industries.</p></p>
