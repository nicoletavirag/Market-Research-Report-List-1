Arterial cannulae are used to access the arteries for direct blood pressure monitoring or to deliver medications. Femoral cannulae are used to access the femoral artery or vein for hemodynamic support. Venous cannulae are used to access veins for different purposes like intravenous delivery of fluids or medications.</p></p>
