The increasing recognition of the importance of inclusivity in gaming, coupled with advancements in technology, is anticipated to drive the market's growth. The integration of features such as advanced haptic feedback, voice control, and eye-tracking technology in adaptive gaming controllers is likely to present new opportunities for market expansion.</p><p>Additionally, the implementation of virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) technologies in gaming is anticipated to propel the demand for adaptive controllers.
