<p><p>The pediatric cranial remolding helmet market refers to the various types of helmets used for the treatment of skull-related conditions in infants and young children. Plagiocephaly helmets are designed to correct asymmetrical head shapes, while brachycephaly helmets address flatness at the back of the head. Scaphocephaly helmets target long and narrow skulls, and craniosynostosis helmets are used for the treatment of premature fusion of the skull bones. These helmets help to reshape the skull by applying gentle pressure as the child's head grows, promoting proper development and symmetry.</p></p>
