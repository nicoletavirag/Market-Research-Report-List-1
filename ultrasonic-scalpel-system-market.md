<p><p>An ultrasonic scalpel system, also known as an ultrasonic surgical aspirator or harmonic scalpel, is a surgical device that utilizes ultrasonic vibrations to cut and coagulate tissues during surgical procedures. This technology offers several advantages over conventional surgical techniques, such as reduced blood loss, shorter procedure times, minimal tissue damage, and improved precision.</p><p>The ultrasonic scalpel system market is experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 11.1% during the forecast period. <p><p>The ultrasonic scalpel system market consists of two main types: gun-type scalpels and scissor-type scalpels. Gun-type scalpels are designed like a gun, with a pistol grip and a trigger that activates the ultrasonic energy. They offer precision cutting and are often used in delicate surgeries. Scissor-type scalpels, on the other hand, have a scissor-like design with two blades that vibrate ultrasonically. These scalpels provide more control and are commonly used in procedures that require dissecting or coagulating tissue. Both types of scalpels utilize ultrasonic energy for efficient and precise surgical cutting.</p></p>
