<p><p>Silicon nitride targets are used in various industries for different purposes. Plane targets are primarily utilized in physical vapor deposition (PVD) processes, where they are mounted perpendicular to the substrate surface. This type of target is commonly employed in the semiconductor industry for thin film deposition. On the other hand, rotating targets are utilized in applications such as magnetron sputtering. They are designed to rotate during deposition, ensuring uniform film formation and longer target life. Rotating targets are often used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics for coating purposes.</p></p>
