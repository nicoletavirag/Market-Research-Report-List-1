<p><p>Automotive semiconductor test equipment market can be divided into two main types: wafer testing and package testing. Wafer testing involves testing the quality and functionality of semiconductor chips at the wafer level before they are packaged. This is done to ensure the chips meet the required standards and perform as expected. Package testing, on the other hand, involves testing the chips after they have been packaged to ensure they are integrated correctly and function properly. Both types of testing are crucial to ensuring the reliability and performance of automotive semiconductors.</p></p>
