Pregelatinized starch is commonly used as a food ingredient in a wide range of products, including soups, sauces, gravies, bakery items, confectioneries, and ready-to-eat meals.</p><p>The global pregelatinized starch market is witnessing significant growth and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 14.4% during the forecast period.
