<p><p>The Single-Dose Prefilled Cartridges market consists of two main types: reusable and disposable. Reusable cartridges can be refilled multiple times with different doses of medication, making them cost-effective and environmentally friendly. They often have a longer lifespan and are easy to clean and maintain. On the other hand, disposable cartridges are designed for single use only and cannot be refilled.
