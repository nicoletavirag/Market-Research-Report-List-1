<p><p>The fixed thermal imagers market consists of three types: fixed mount, rotary, and non-rotary. Fixed mount thermal imagers are stationary devices installed in a specific location for continuous monitoring. Rotary fixed thermal imagers can be rotated horizontally or vertically to cover a larger area. Non-rotary fixed thermal imagers have a fixed field of view and cannot be rotated. Handheld fixed thermal imagers are portable devices that can be operated by hand. Each type serves different purposes in applications such as surveillance, industrial inspections, and firefighting.</p></p>
