<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The mineral insulated thermocouples market includes three types: grounded, ungrounded, and exposed. Grounded thermocouples have the conductors welded to the metal sheath, providing better response time and accuracy. Ungrounded thermocouples have insulated conductors, reducing electrical noise, but increasing response time. Exposed thermocouples have a naked bead or junction, enabling faster response time but with lower accuracy and durability. These types cater to different applications, with grounded thermocouples suitable for precise measurements, ungrounded ones for noisy environments, and exposed thermocouples for rapid temperature changes.</p></p>
