<p><p>The airborne photoelectric targeting system market is categorized into two types: multispectral and hyperspectral. Multispectral systems capture images in multiple spectral bands, enabling detection and identification of targets based on their unique spectral signatures. These systems are commonly used in defense and surveillance applications. Hyperspectral systems, on the other hand, capture images across hundreds of narrow and contiguous spectral bands, allowing for precise identification and analysis of materials and objects. This technology finds applications in environmental monitoring, agriculture, and mineral exploration, among others.</p></p>
