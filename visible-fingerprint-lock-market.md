<p><p>The visible fingerprint lock market consists of two main types: optical fingerprint locks and semiconductor fingerprint locks. Optical fingerprint locks use light to capture the image of a fingerprint and convert it into a digital representation for authentication. On the other hand, semiconductor fingerprint locks use small sensor arrays to detect the electrical currents generated by a fingerprint, which are then converted into a unique identifier. Both types offer secure and convenient access control solutions for various applications.</p></p>
