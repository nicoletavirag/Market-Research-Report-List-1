<p><p>The oat flour market consists of two main types - organic oat flour and normal oat flour. Organic oat flour is produced from oats that have been grown without the use of synthetic fertilizers, pesticides, or genetically modified organisms. It is often preferred by health-conscious consumers due to its perceived higher nutritional value and environmental benefits. On the other hand, normal oat flour is made from conventionally grown oats that may have been exposed to synthetic chemicals during cultivation. This type of oat flour is typically more affordable and widely available in the market.</p></p>
