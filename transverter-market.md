<p><p>Transverter market types can be classified under open loop and closed loop systems. The open loop market involves a system where the output is not directly controlled by the input, allowing for flexibility and adaptability to changing conditions. On the other hand, closed loop market involves a system where the output is controlled by the input, providing a more stable and predictable performance. Both types of markets have their own advantages and limitations, catering to different needs and requirements of customers.</p></p>
