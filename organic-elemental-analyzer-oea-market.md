<p><p>The Organic Elemental Analyzer (OEA) market consists of various types: GC Chromatography, Frontal Chromatography, and Adsorption-Desorption. GC Chromatography involves the separation of organic compounds based on their interaction with a stationary phase. Frontal Chromatography utilizes a sample's differential affinity for a stationary phase to separate components. Adsorption-Desorption involves measuring the amount of gas adsorbed or desorbed on a solid surface. These techniques are used in the OEA market to analyze the elemental composition of organic samples, providing valuable insights for research, quality control, and environmental analysis.</p></p>
