<p><p>Heart rate monitoring devices are used to measure the heart rate during physical activities and exercise. Wrist watch heart rate monitors are worn on the wrist and use optical sensors to detect the pulse rate. Chest strap heart rate monitors consist of a strap worn around the chest, with a sensor that detects the heart rate. Strapless heart rate monitors use optical sensors and are worn around the arm or finger. These devices help individuals track their heart rate and optimize their workouts for better fitness and health.</p></p>
