<p><ul><li>Southbridge</li><li>Northbridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The computer chip market is divided into different types, including the Southbridge and Northbridge markets. The Southbridge chip is responsible for managing peripheral devices such as hard drives, USB ports, and audio controllers. It coordinates data flow between these devices and the computer's central processing unit (CPU). On the other hand, the Northbridge chip handles communication between the CPU, memory, and graphics card. It optimizes data transfer speeds within the system, ensuring efficient performance. Southbridge and Northbridge chips play crucial roles in the smooth functioning of a computer system.</p></p>
