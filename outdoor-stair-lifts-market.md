<p><p>Outdoor stair lifts are mobility devices designed to help individuals with limited mobility navigate outdoor stairs. There are two main types of outdoor stair lifts: straight and curved. Curved stair lifts, on the other hand, are specifically designed to fit on staircases with curves or bends.
