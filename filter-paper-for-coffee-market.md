<p><p>Filter paper for coffee is available in two main market types: non-heat-sealable and heat-sealable. Non-heat-sealable filter paper is not suitable for sealing with heat, but it offers excellent filtration properties. It is commonly used in drip coffee makers and pour-over brewing methods. On the other hand, heat-sealable filter paper can be sealed with heat, making it ideal for use in coffee capsules and other single-serve brewing systems. Both types are chosen based on specific brewing requirements, with non-heat-sealable preferred for traditional brewing methods and heat-sealable for convenience-focused systems.</p></p>
