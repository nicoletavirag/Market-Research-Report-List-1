<p><p>Wrap around case packers are machines used in packaging industries to pack products in cases or cartons. There are two main types: automatic wrap around case packers and fully automatic wrap around case packers. </p><p>Automatic wrap around case packers are semi-automated machines that require some human intervention. They require operators to manually place products into the cases, while the machine automatically folds and seals the cases.</p><p>In contrast, fully automatic wrap around case packers are completely automated machines. They can pick up products from a conveyor belt or other feed systems and place them accurately into the cases, without human intervention. These machines can also automatically fold and seal the cases, increasing efficiency and reducing labor costs.</p></p>
