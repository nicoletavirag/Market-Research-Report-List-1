<p><p>The PCB board terminals market offers various types of connectors for different applications. Screw type terminals are commonly used for strong and secure connections, where the wires are clamped down by screws. Plug-in terminals allow for easy and quick installation by inserting the wires directly into the connector. Other market options include spring-type terminals, which use spring mechanisms to hold the wires in place, and insulation displacement terminals, which pierce through the insulation of the wire to establish a connection. Each type offers its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
