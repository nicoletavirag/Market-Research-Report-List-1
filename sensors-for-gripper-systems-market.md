<p><p>The Sensors for Gripper Systems market includes various types of sensors used in gripper systems for industrial applications. Piezoelectric sensors are capable of converting mechanical pressure or strain into electrical signals, providing accurate force measurement. Torque sensors enable the measurement of rotational forces applied by grippers, ensuring precise gripping control. Other types of sensors used in gripper systems include proximity sensors, optical sensors, magnetic sensors, and ultrasonic sensors, which offer different sensing capabilities for detecting the presence or absence of objects, measuring distances, or detecting specific materials. These sensors play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and accuracy of gripper systems in industrial settings.</p></p>
