Heat treatment salts are used to enhance the properties of metals through processes like hardening or tempering. Paint strippers/removers are chemicals used to remove paint or coatings from metal surfaces. Cleaners and degreasers are used to remove dirt, oil, and other contaminants from metal surfaces. Other types of metal processing chemicals include pickling solutions, inhibitors, and plating chemicals.</p></p>
