C-Band LNBs receive signals between 3.7 to 4.2 GHz, Ku-Band LNBs receive signals between 10.7 to 12.75 GHz, Ka-Band LNBs receive signals between 17.7 to 21.2 GHz, and X-Band LNBs receive signals between 8.0 to 12.4 GHz. These different market types cater to different frequency requirements and applications in satellite communication systems.</p></p>
