<p><p>Semiconductor heat treatment furnaces are used in the manufacturing process of semiconductors for various applications. The horizontal furnace is a type of semiconductor heat treatment furnace where wafers are horizontally placed on a rotating quartz boat. This allows for uniform heating and cooling of the wafers. On the other hand, the vertical furnace is another type where wafers are placed vertically in a tube and heated using a filament or a radiant heating element. Both types of furnaces are crucial in the semiconductor industry for achieving precise and controlled heating conditions.</p></p>
