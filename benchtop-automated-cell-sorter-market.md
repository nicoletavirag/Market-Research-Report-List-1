<p><p>The benchtop automated cell sorter market includes several types of sorting technologies. The single cell sorter separates individual cells based on specific characteristics or phenotypes. The fluorescent activated cell sorter separates cells labeled with fluorescent markers to identify and isolate specific cell populations. The magnetic cell sorter uses magnetic beads to separate cells labeled with specific antibodies. The buoyancy activated cell sorter separates cells using the density differences between different cell types. These technologies enable researchers to efficiently and precisely isolate specific cells for further analysis and experimentation.</p></p>
