<p><p>The fuel flexible boiler market includes various types of boilers that can efficiently burn different types of fuels. Circulating Fluidised Bed Combustion (CFBC) boiler technology involves the combustion of solid fuels in a bed of limestone, helping reduce emissions. Atmospheric Fluidised Bed Combustion (AFBC) boiler uses low velocity air to burn a variety of solid fuels. Multi fuel firing boiler is capable of burning multiple types of fuels simultaneously, providing flexibility and efficiency in fuel utilization. These technologies offer solutions for diverse fuel sources while ensuring environmental sustainability.</p></p>
