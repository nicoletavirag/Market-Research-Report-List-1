<p><p>Pushbutton locks are categorized into two main market types: electronic pushbutton locks and mechanical pushbutton locks. </p><p>Electronic pushbutton locks utilize electronic components to provide access control. They often feature keypads where users enter a numerical code to unlock the door. These locks can offer advanced features such as remote control access and integration with security systems. </p><p>On the other hand, mechanical pushbutton locks rely on purely mechanical mechanisms for operation. Users enter a specific combination of buttons to unlock the door. These locks are usually simpler in design and don't require any electrical components or power source.</p></p>
