<p><strong>The Medium Voltage Instrument Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LCT</li><li>LVT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medium Voltage Instrument Transformers market can be categorized into two types: LCT (Low Current Transformers) and LVT (Low Voltage Transformers). LCT transformers are designed to handle low currents in the medium voltage range, typically up to 10 kV. They are used to reduce high-voltage currents for measurement or protection purposes in electrical systems. On the other hand, LVT transformers are utilized to step down high voltages to low voltages, usually up to 600V, for various applications like industrial machinery and utility grids.</p></p>
