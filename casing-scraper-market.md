<p><strong>The Casing Scraper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Rotational</li><li>Rotational</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Casing scrapers are used in the oil and gas industry to clean the inside of well casings. The market for casing scrapers can be divided into two types: non-rotational and rotational. Non-rotational casing scrapers are designed to be pulled through the casing using a wireline or slickline. They are primarily used for light cleaning and removing debris. On the other hand, rotational casing scrapers are used in conjunction with a drill string and are capable of rotating while cleaning. They offer enhanced cleaning capabilities and are capable of removing stubborn deposits such as scale or cement.</p></p>
