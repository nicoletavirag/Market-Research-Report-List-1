<p><ul><li>Two Mirror System</li><li>Three Mirror System</li><li>Four Mirror System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kaleidoscope market types include the Two Mirror System, Three Mirror System, Four Mirror System, and Others. In the Two Mirror System, two mirrors are placed opposite each other, forming a long, narrow tube. When the user looks through one end, colorful patterns are created by the reflection of objects inside. Similarly, the Three Mirror System utilizes three mirrors to create more complex and intricate patterns. The Four Mirror System, employing four mirrors, produces even more intricate symmetrical patterns. Lastly, the Others category comprises various kaleidoscope designs that may deviate from the typical mirror configurations, offering unique visual experiences.</p></p>
