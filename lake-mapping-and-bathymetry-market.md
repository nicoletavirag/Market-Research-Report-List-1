<p><strong>The Lake Mapping and Bathymetry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contour Map</li><li>3D Bathymetric Map</li><li>Sedimentation Map</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lake mapping and bathymetry market types include contour maps, 3D bathymetric maps, and sedimentation maps. A contour map represents depth changes in a lake using contour lines. These lines connect points of equal depth, allowing for the visualization of underwater topography. A 3D bathymetric map provides a more detailed representation by using advanced technologies like sonar to create a three-dimensional model of the lake floor. Sedimentation maps focus on identifying and mapping the distribution of sediments in the lake, helping understand ecological processes and sediment-related issues. These maps aid in various industries, including navigation, hydrology, and environmental management.</p></p>
