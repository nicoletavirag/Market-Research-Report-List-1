<p><p>Urological operating tables come in various types based on their operating mechanisms. Electric tables are powered by electricity and offer precise positioning. Hydraulic tables use hydraulic fluid to adjust height and position. Electro-hydraulic tables combine both electric and hydraulic systems for optimal control. Manual tables are operated manually by healthcare staff. Each type of table has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the urological procedure being performed.</p></p>
