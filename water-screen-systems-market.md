<p><p>The Water Screen Systems market consists of two main types, namely Traveling Water Screen Systems and Static Water Screen Systems. Traveling Water Screen Systems are commonly used in industries to remove debris and solids from water sources. They are designed with a continuous belt that moves through the water, capturing and removing impurities. On the other hand, Static Water Screen Systems are stationary screens that use a mesh or perforated plate to filter out debris from water sources. Both systems play a crucial role in maintaining clean and clear water for various industrial applications.</p></p>
