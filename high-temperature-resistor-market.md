<p><strong>The High Temperature Resistor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-In-Line</li><li>Dual-In-Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Temperature Resistor market includes two main types, namely Single-In-Line (SIL) and Dual-In-Line (DIL). SIL resistors are cylindrical in shape and have one terminal at each end, making them suitable for through-hole mounting on printed circuit boards (PCBs). DIL resistors, on the other hand, have two terminals on each side, enabling them to be mounted on both sides of the PCB. Both types are designed to withstand high temperatures, ensuring reliable performance in demanding applications.</p></p>
