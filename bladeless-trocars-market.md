<p><p>Bladeless trocars are surgical instruments used in minimally invasive surgeries to create a safe entry point for other surgical instruments. Unlike traditional trocars, which use a sharp blade to puncture the skin, bladeless trocars use a blunt tip with a spring-loaded shield. This design reduces the risk of injuries to internal organs and blood vessels, leading to improved patient safety and faster recovery.</p><p>The bladeless trocars market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing adoption of minimally invasive surgeries globally. Factors such as reduced post-operative pain, faster recovery time, and smaller incisions are driving the demand for bladeless trocars. Additionally, rising healthcare expenditure, technological advancements, and the growing prevalence of chronic diseases are fueling market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated the adoption of minimally invasive surgeries, as these procedures require shorter hospital stays and reduce the risk of viral exposure.
