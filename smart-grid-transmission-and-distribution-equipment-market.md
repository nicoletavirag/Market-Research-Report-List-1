<p><p>The Smart Grid Transmission and Distribution Equipment market consists of various types of advanced technologies. High-Voltage Direct Current (HVDC) enables efficient long-distance electricity transmission by converting alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC). Flexible AC Transmission System (FACTS) devices enhance grid stability, control power flow, and improve voltage quality. Synchrophasors provide real-time information on power system conditions by measuring voltage and current waveforms. Together, these technologies optimize grid operations, increase reliability, and enable better integration of renewable energy sources for a sustainable energy future.</p></p>
