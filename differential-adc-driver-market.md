<p><strong>The Differential ADC Driver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-ended</li><li>Pseudo-differential</li><li>Fully-differential</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Differential ADC Driver market comprises three main types: Single-ended, Pseudo-differential, and Fully-differential. Single-ended drivers are commonly used in low-cost applications where the signal source is referenced to ground. Pseudo-differential drivers are employed when a differential output is required for noise reduction, but the common-mode voltage is unknown. Fully-differential drivers offer the most accurate representation of the input signal, as they provide a true differential output and are employed in high-performance applications. These driver types cater to various market demands and specific requirements for signal processing and noise reduction.</p></p>
