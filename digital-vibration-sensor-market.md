<p><p>The digital vibration sensor market is categorized into three types: 3-Axis, Programmable, and Others. </p><p>1. 3-Axis sensors can detect and measure vibrations across all three axes (x, y, and z) simultaneously, providing comprehensive data for analysis and monitoring of vibrations in multiple directions.</p><p>2. Programmable sensors allow customization of vibration detection parameters and settings, offering flexibility and adaptability to suit different applications and environments. Users can modify sensitivity, frequency ranges, and other parameters as per their specific requirements.</p><p>3. The "Others" category encompasses different types of digital vibration sensors that may not fit into the 3-Axis or Programmable classifications, such as those with specialized features or unique applications.</p></p>
