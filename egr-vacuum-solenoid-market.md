<p><p>The EGR vacuum solenoid market consists of two main types: Universal Direct Acting and Semi-Direct Acting. Universal Direct Acting solenoids are designed to directly control the EGR valve by regulating the vacuum to open or close it. On the other hand, Semi-Direct Acting solenoids function indirectly by controlling another component that then affects the EGR valve. Both types play a crucial role in managing exhaust gas recirculation, but they differ in their approach to controlling the EGR valve.</p></p>
