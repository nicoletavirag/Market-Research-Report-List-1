There are two types of AGSS markets - Single Blower and Double Blower. In the Single Blower market, there is only one blower unit responsible for evacuating waste gases, while in the Double Blower market, two blower units work together to efficiently remove waste gases. Both types offer different solutions for gas scavenging, catering to the specific needs of healthcare facilities.</p></p>
