<p><p>The Split-Body Ball Valves market consists of two main types: Flange Type and Butt Welding Type. Flange Type valves are designed with flanges on both ends, allowing for easy installation and removal. They are commonly used in applications that require frequent maintenance. Butt Welding Type valves, on the other hand, are welded directly to the pipes, providing a sturdier and more secure connection. These valves are typically used in high-pressure and high-temperature applications where leakage prevention is crucial. Both types serve specific purposes and offer distinct advantages in various industrial settings.</p></p>
