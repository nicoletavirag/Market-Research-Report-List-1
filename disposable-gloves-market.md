<p><p>The disposable gloves market consists of three main types: latex, vinyl, and nitrile gloves. Latex gloves are made from natural rubber and are known for their excellent flexibility and tactile sensitivity. Vinyl gloves, on the other hand, are made from synthetic materials and are more affordable but may not provide as much durability. Nitrile gloves are synthetic as well, but they offer enhanced chemical resistance and strength, making them ideal for industries like healthcare and laboratory settings. All three types cater to the need for disposable gloves in various industries.</p></p>
