<p><p>The strainers market comprises various types of strainers that serve different purposes. Y Type Strainers are designed to remove debris and solid particles from pipelines. Basket Strainers feature a removable basket that can be cleaned or replaced to maintain optimal flow. Duplex Strainers contain two parallel strainers that allow continuous operation even during cleaning. Other types of strainers include T Type, Temporary, and Tee Strainers, which are used in specific applications depending on the requirements. Each strainer type plays a crucial role in ensuring the smooth and uninterrupted flow of fluids in industrial processes.</p></p>
