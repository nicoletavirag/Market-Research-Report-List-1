<p><p>Swivel joints are crucial components used in various industries, allowing rotational movement between two pipes or hoses. The straight port type swivel joint consists of two pipes connected in a straight line, providing rotation without deviation. The elbow port type swivel joint enables rotational movement in pipes at an angle. Multiple port type swivel joints have more than two ports, allowing rotation between multiple pipes simultaneously. Each market type caters to different needs, providing specific solutions for industries requiring flexible movement and connection between pipes or hoses.</p></p>
