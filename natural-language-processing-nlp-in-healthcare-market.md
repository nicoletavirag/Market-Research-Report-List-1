<p><p>Natural Language Processing (NLP) in Healthcare encompasses three main market types: Rule-Based NLP, Statistically Based NLP, and Mixed NLP. Rule-Based NLP relies on predefined rules and patterns for processing and extracting information from text. Statistically Based NLP uses statistical models and algorithms to analyze language patterns and make predictions. Mixed NLP combines elements of both rule-based and statistically based approaches to provide a more comprehensive and accurate analysis of healthcare data. Each market type offers unique advantages and is utilized in various healthcare settings for improved patient care and research.</p></p>
