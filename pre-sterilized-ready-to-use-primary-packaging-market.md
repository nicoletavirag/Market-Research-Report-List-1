<p><p>Pre-Sterilized/Ready-to-Use Primary Packaging refers to packaging materials that have been sterilized before use, eliminating the need for additional sterilization processes. In the pharmaceutical industry, this packaging is available in three main types: cartridges, syringes, and vials. Cartridges are cylindrical containers used for storing and delivering liquid or powdered medications. Syringes are devices used for injecting small amounts of medication, with a barrel, plunger, and needle. Vials are small glass or plastic containers used to store liquids, typically medications, and are sealed with a rubber stopper.</p></p>
