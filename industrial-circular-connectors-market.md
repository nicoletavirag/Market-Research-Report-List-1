<p><p>The industrial circular connectors market includes various types such as cable mount, flange mount, panel mount, and PCB mount connectors. Cable mount connectors are used for soldering or crimping the cable directly onto the connector. Flange mount connectors are fixed onto the equipment's chassis with screws. Panel mount connectors are mounted on the equipment's panel and have a threaded locking mechanism. PCB mount connectors are attached directly to the printed circuit board during the manufacturing process. These connectors provide reliable and secure connections for various industrial applications.</p></p>
