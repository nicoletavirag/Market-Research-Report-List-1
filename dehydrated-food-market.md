<p><strong>The Dehydrated Food Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Dried</li><li>Freeze Dried</li><li>Vacuum Dried</li><li>Sun Dried</li><li>Hot Air Dried</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dehydrated food market consists of various types of drying techniques. Spray dried refers to the process of swiftly drying the food particles with hot air and forming powdered flakes. Freeze dried involves freezing the food and using vacuum and heat to remove moisture, resulting in light and crispy texture. Vacuum dried uses low pressure and temperature to remove moisture from food. Sun dried involves leaving the food under sunlight to naturally dry out. Hot air dried utilizes hot air or ovens to extract moisture. Other methods may include drum drying or microwave drying.</p></p>
