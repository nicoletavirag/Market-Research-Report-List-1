<p><strong>The Conical Implant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Root Implant</li><li>Plate Dental Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conical implant market consists of two main types: root implants and plate dental implants. Root implants are designed to mimic a natural tooth's structure, with a tapered shape that fits into the jawbone and a crown on top. They provide stability and support for prosthetic teeth. Plate dental implants, on the other hand, are flat and wider, resembling a plate. They are used when the jawbone is shallow or thin and need to distribute the load over a broader area. Both types aim to provide effective solutions for dental restorations and improve oral function.</p></p>
