<p><p>Paramotors are powered paragliders that have two distinct market types: 2-stroke paramotors and 4-stroke paramotors. The 2-stroke paramotor market refers to those that utilize a two-stroke engine, which provides a lightweight and simpler design, making it more suitable for recreational use. On the other hand, the 4-stroke paramotor market involves paramotors equipped with a four-stroke engine, offering increased fuel efficiency, quieter operation, and longer service life. These features make 4-stroke paramotors more popular among professional pilots or those seeking a more sophisticated and robust paramotor experience.</p></p>
