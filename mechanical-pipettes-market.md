<p><strong>The Mechanical Pipettes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Channel Pipette</li><li>Multi-Channel Pipette</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical pipettes are widely used in laboratories for accurate and precise liquid handling. The single-channel pipette market refers to the market for mechanical pipettes that are designed to aspirate and dispense liquids in a single channel at a time. These pipettes are commonly used when working with small volumes of liquids. On the other hand, the multi-channel pipette market caters to pipettes that are designed to aspirate and dispense liquids simultaneously from multiple channels, thus increasing efficiency and throughput. Multi-channel pipettes are frequently used when working with high-throughput assays or when multiple samples need to be processed simultaneously.</p></p>
