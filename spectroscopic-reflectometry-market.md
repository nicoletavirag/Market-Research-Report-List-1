<p><strong>The Spectroscopic Reflectometry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic Spectral Ellipsometer</li><li>Imaging Ellipsometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spectroscopic Reflectometry involves two types of instruments: Automatic Spectral Ellipsometer and Imaging Ellipsometer. An Automatic Spectral Ellipsometer is a device that measures the change in polarization of light as it interacts with a sample to determine its optical properties, such as thickness and refractive index. On the other hand, an Imaging Ellipsometer combines ellipsometry with imaging techniques to provide spatially resolved information about a sample's optical properties across its surface. Both instruments are used in various industries, including semiconductors, optics, and thin film coatings.</p></p>
