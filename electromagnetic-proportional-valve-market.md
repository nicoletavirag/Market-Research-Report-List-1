<p><p>The electromagnetic proportional valve market can be categorized into two main types: direct-acting type and pilot-operated type. </p><p>The direct-acting type of valve operates by directly controlling the flow rate of fluid without the need for an external energy source. It is suitable for applications requiring low flow rates and has the advantage of providing accurate flow control.</p><p>On the other hand, the pilot-operated type of valve uses a smaller pilot valve to control the main valve. This type is commonly used in applications where high flow rates are required, as it offers greater force and flow capacity.</p><p>Both types have their own advantages and are utilized in different industrial sectors depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
