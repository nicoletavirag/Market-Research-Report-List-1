<p><p>Tie rod cylinders are mechanical devices used in various industries to provide linear motion. Hydraulic tie rod cylinders use fluid power to generate force and motion, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications that require significant force. Pneumatic tie rod cylinders, on the other hand, are powered by compressed air and are commonly used in lighter-duty applications. Both types of cylinders play a crucial role in industrial automation, machinery, and construction, offering efficient and precise movement control.</p></p>
