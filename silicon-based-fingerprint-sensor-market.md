<p><p>The silicon-based fingerprint sensor market consists of two main types: touch type and slide type. Touch type sensors require users to place their finger firmly on the sensor to capture the fingerprint impression. They are commonly used in smartphones and tablets. On the other hand, slide type sensors require users to swipe their finger across the sensor's surface. These sensors are often used in applications such as access control systems or fingerprint identification devices, where higher security is required due to the more accurate and detailed fingerprint image they can capture.</p></p>
