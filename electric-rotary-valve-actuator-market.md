<p><p>The Electric Rotary Valve Actuator market can be categorized into two types: Directly Attached Type and Pedestal Type. In a Directly Attached Type, the actuator is directly attached to the valve, providing a compact and efficient solution. This type is commonly used for small to medium-sized valves. On the other hand, the Pedestal Type actuator is mounted on a separate pedestal or base, suitable for large and heavy-duty valves. Both types offer reliable and precise control of valve operations, catering to different valve sizes and applications.</p></p>
