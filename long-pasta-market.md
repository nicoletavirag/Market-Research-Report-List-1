<p><p>Long pasta, such as Fusilli, Spaghetti, Capellini, Bucatini, and others, can be classified into different market types based on their characteristics and usage. Spaghetti is the most popular long pasta variety, known for its thin cylindrical shape, versatile nature, and ability to pair with various sauces. Capellini, also known as angel hair pasta, is thinner and delicate, best suited for light sauces. Bucatini is a thicker pasta with a hollow center, perfect for hearty sauces. Other long pasta varieties cater to specific preferences, such as linguine, pappardelle, and tagliatelle.</p></p>
