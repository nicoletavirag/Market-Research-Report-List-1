<p><p>Metal forming in the automotive market consists of various types such as roll forming, stretch forming, stamping, deep drawing, hydroforming, and others. Roll forming involves shaping metal through a series of rollers. Stretch forming stretches a metal sheet over a die to achieve complex shapes. Stamping uses a die to cut or shape metal. Deep drawing involves stretching a metal sheet into a mold. Hydroforming uses high-pressure fluid to shape metal. Other methods include processes like bending, spinning, and forging.</p></p>
