<p><p>Energy efficient windows are designed to minimize heat loss and reduce energy consumption. Double glazing windows consist of two glass layers separated by an insulating gas, which enhances thermal performance and reduces condensation. Triple Low-E glazing windows have an additional inner glass layer coated with a special Low-E coating, further enhancing energy efficiency by reflecting heat back into the room. Both types of windows provide better insulation, reduce energy bills, and improve comfort by maintaining consistent room temperatures.</p></p>
