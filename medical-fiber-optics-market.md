<p><ul><li>Silica Fiber/Glass Fiber Optics</li><li>Plastic Fiber Optics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical fiber optics market is categorized into two main types: silica fiber/glass fiber optics and plastic fiber optics. Silica fiber/glass fiber optics are made from high-quality silica glass and are extensively used in medical devices for imaging and illumination purposes due to their excellent light transmission properties. On the other hand, plastic fiber optics are made from plastic polymers and offer several advantages such as flexibility and cost-effectiveness. They are commonly used in medical applications where a smaller fiber size is required, such as endoscopy and dental devices.</p></p>
