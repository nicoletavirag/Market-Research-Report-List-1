<p><p>Branch outlets are fittings that provide a junction or connection point in a pipeline system. They come in three main types: threaded, socket-weld, and butt-weld. Threaded branch outlets have threads on one end for connection, socket-weld branch outlets have a socket for insertion and welding, while butt-weld branch outlets have a smooth surface for seamless welding. These market types cater to different industrial needs and preferences, providing options for secure, leak-free connections in various pipelines.</p></p>
