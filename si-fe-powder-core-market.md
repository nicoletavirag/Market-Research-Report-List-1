<p><p>The Si-Fe powder core market can be categorized into three types based on the magnetic permeability (µ) of the cores: below 60 µ, 60 µ ~ 90 µ, and above 90 µ. In the below 60 µ market, the cores have relatively low magnetic permeability and are suitable for applications that require low losses at high frequencies. The 60 µ ~ 90 µ market includes cores with medium magnetic permeability, making them ideal for general-purpose applications. The above 90 µ market consists of cores with high magnetic permeability, which are commonly used in applications requiring high inductance and low losses.</p></p>
