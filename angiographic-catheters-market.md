<p><p>The angiographic catheters market consists of various types of catheters used in angiographic procedures. Cutting balloon catheters are designed to treat narrowed or blocked blood vessels by using small blades on the balloon surface. Drug-eluting balloon catheters are used to deliver medication directly to the affected area to prevent restenosis. Scoring balloon catheters have tiny grooves on their surface to help create controlled injuries to the plaque. Conventional balloon catheters are the standard type used to inflate and deflate during procedures, assisting in opening blocked arteries.</p></p>
