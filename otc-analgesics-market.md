<p><p>OTC analgesics, also known as over-the-counter analgesics, are medications that provide effective and temporary relief from pain and discomfort without a prescription. Companies are introducing innovative delivery methods such as transdermal patches, sprays, and gels to enhance absorption and provide targeted pain relief.</p><p>The OTC analgesics market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.6% during the forecast period.
