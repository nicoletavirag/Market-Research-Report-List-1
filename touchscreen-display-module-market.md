<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Touchscreen Display Module market consists of two major types: Capacitive Touchscreen Display Module and Resistive Touchscreen Display Module. Capacitive modules are widely used due to their high sensitivity and superior touch accuracy. They are based on the principle of electrical conductivity and respond to the electrical charge of human touch. Resistive modules, on the other hand, rely on pressure applied to the screen. While they are less sensitive and precise compared to capacitive modules, they offer durability and are suitable for applications requiring interaction with gloves or stylus.</p></p>
