<p><p>The firefighting helmets market consists of two main types: full face coverage type and half face helmet type. Full face coverage helmets provide complete protection to the firefighter's head and face, shielding them from heat, impacts, and other potential hazards. They typically include a visor or face shield to offer additional protection. On the other hand, half face helmet types only cover half of the firefighter's face, leaving the lower face and chin exposed. Despite not offering complete coverage, they still provide a certain level of protection and are often utilized for specific firefighting tasks.</p></p>
