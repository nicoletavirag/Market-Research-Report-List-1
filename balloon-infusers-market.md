<p><ul><li>Continuous Rate Pumps</li><li>Variable Rate Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Balloon Infusers market offers two types of pumps: Continuous Rate Pumps and Variable Rate Pumps. Continuous Rate Pumps are designed to infuse a consistent rate of fluid into the balloon. They are ideal for applications where a constant infusion rate is required. On the other hand, Variable Rate Pumps allow for adjustable infusion rates, offering flexibility in the amount of fluid infused into the balloon. They are suitable for situations where the required infusion rate can change over time. These pumps cater to different needs in the market, providing options to suit various medical requirements.</p></p>
