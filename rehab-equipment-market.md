<p><p>Rehab equipment market includes two main types: upper body rehabilitation equipment and lower body rehabilitation equipment. Upper body rehabilitation equipment is designed to aid in the recovery and strengthening of the upper limbs, including arms, shoulders, and hands. This type of equipment may include hand exercisers, shoulder pulleys, and arm cycling machines. On the other hand, lower body rehabilitation equipment focuses on the lower limbs, such as legs, hips, and feet. Examples of this equipment include treadmills, exercise bikes, and resistance bands. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in assisting individuals with physical therapy and rehabilitation process.</p></p>
