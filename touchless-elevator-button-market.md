Mobile app-based systems allow users to control elevator functions through a smartphone app, eliminating the need for physical buttons. The "others" category includes alternative touchless technologies like gesture recognition or voice commands. These touchless solutions aim to provide a safer and more hygienic elevator experience by reducing the need for physical contact.</p></p>
