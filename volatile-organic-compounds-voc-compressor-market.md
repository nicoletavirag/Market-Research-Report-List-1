<p><p>The Volatile Organic Compounds (VOC) Compressor market consists of two main types: Rotary Compressor and Scroll Compressor. A Rotary Compressor is a type of VOC compressor that uses rotary motion to compress the gas. It works by trapping gas in the space between rotating blades and the compressor housing. On the other hand, a Scroll Compressor uses a scroll-shaped component to compress the gas. It operates by creating a continuous flow of gas between fixed and moving spiral-shaped components, resulting in efficient compression of VOCs.</p></p>
