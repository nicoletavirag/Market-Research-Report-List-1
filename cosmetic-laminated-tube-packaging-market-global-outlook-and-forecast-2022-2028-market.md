There are two main types of laminated tubes used in this market: ABL (Aluminum Barrier Laminate) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminate). ABL tubes are made with multiple layers including aluminum for enhanced protection, while PBL tubes are composed of different plastic layers for barrier properties. Both types of tubes are widely used in the cosmetic industry for packaging various products.</p></p>
