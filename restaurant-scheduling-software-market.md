<p><p>Restaurant scheduling software can be categorized into two market types: cloud-based and web-based. It provides greater flexibility, accessibility, and scalability as it can be accessed from anywhere with an internet connection. On the other hand, web-based software is installed on local servers or computers and can only be accessed on those specific devices. Though it lacks the flexibility of cloud-based software, it offers greater control and security to the users.</p></p>
