<p><strong>The Water Pipeline Leak Detection System (LDS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Continuously</li><li>Continuously With External Measurements</li><li>Continuously With Internal Measurements</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Water Pipeline Leak Detection System (LDS) market consists of three types: non-continuously, continuously with external measurements, and continuously with internal measurements. Non-continuously refers to systems that detect leaks by periodically monitoring the pipeline. Continuously with external measurements involves using sensors placed externally on the pipeline to monitor for potential leaks. Continuously with internal measurements employs internal sensors to constantly monitor the pipeline for any signs of leakage. These different market types provide varying levels of leak detection efficiency and can be chosen based on specific requirements and budget constraints.</p></p>
