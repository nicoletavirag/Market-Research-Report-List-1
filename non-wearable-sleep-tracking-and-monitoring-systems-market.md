<p><p>Non-wearable sleep tracking and monitoring systems are divided into two market types: separate systems and integrated systems. Separate systems consist of devices that are independent and placed separately from the user during sleep. These devices use sensors to track and monitor sleep patterns. Integrated systems, on the other hand, are embedded into existing products like mattresses or pillows, eliminating the need for separate devices. These systems incorporate sensors into the sleep surface itself, providing accurate sleep tracking and monitoring without additional devices.</p></p>
