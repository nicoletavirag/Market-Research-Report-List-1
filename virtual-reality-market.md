<p><p>The virtual reality market can be categorized into three types based on the level of immersion: non-immersive technology, semi-immersive technology, and fully immersive technology. Non-immersive technology refers to systems that provide a limited virtual experience, often via a computer screen or projection. Semi-immersive technology offers a higher level of immersion, typically incorporating a head-mounted display with a wider field of view. Fully immersive technology provides the most immersive experience, combining head-mounted displays with motion tracking, audio, and sometimes physical feedback, enabling users to feel completely immersed in a virtual environment.</p></p>
