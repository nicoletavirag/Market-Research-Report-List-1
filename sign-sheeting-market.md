<p><p>Sign sheeting is used in road signs and traffic control devices to ensure visibility and reflectivity. Two common types in the market are glass bead type and micro prismatic type. Glass bead type sheeting uses glass beads to reflect light, while micro prismatic type sheeting uses tiny prisms to reflect light in multiple directions. Glass bead type sheeting is commonly used for temporary signs, while micro prismatic type sheeting is more durable and long-lasting, making it suitable for permanent signs and high-traffic areas.</p></p>
