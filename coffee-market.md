<p><p>There are three main types of coffee markets: roasted coffee, soluble coffee, and coffee pods. Roasted coffee refers to the traditional form where green coffee beans are roasted and ground before being brewed. Soluble coffee, also known as instant coffee, is made by drying and crushing brewed coffee, creating a powder that dissolves in water. Coffee pods are convenient single-serving units that are pre-packaged with ground coffee and can be used with specialized coffee machines. These different market types cater to various consumer preferences and convenience needs.</p></p>
