<p><p>A metal floating dock is a platform made of metal materials that allows watercraft and vessels to be securely moored, loaded, and unloaded. These docks are designed to float on the water's surface without the need for permanent attachment to the shoreline. They are commonly used in marinas, ports, recreational areas, and other waterfront locations.</p><p>The metal floating dock market has been experiencing steady growth in recent years due to the increasing demand for water-based recreational activities, surging investments in marina and port infrastructures, and rising disposable incomes.
