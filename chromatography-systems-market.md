<p><p>Chromatography Systems market comprises various types such as Gas Chromatography (GC), Liquid Chromatography (LC), LC-Mass Spectrometry (LC-MS), and GC-Mass Spectrometry (GC-MS). Gas Chromatography utilizes a gaseous mobile phase to separate and analyze volatile compounds, while Liquid Chromatography employs a liquid mobile phase. LC-MS combines the separating power of LC with the detection capabilities of Mass Spectrometry, enabling identification and quantification of complex samples. GC-MS integrates gas chromatography with mass spectrometry for enhanced analysis of volatile compounds. These systems find applications in industries like pharmaceuticals, food & beverages, forensics, and environmental sciences.</p></p>
