<p><p>The Aircraft Personal Locator Beacon (PLB) market can be classified into three types: 406MHz Locator Beacons, 121.5MHz Locator Beacons, and Other Locator Beacons. 406MHz Locator Beacons transmit distress signals to the global search and rescue satellites, providing accurate and reliable location information. 121.5MHz Locator Beacons, although less reliable and being phased out, transmit signals to nearby aircraft and ground stations. Other Locator Beacons include alternative technologies and frequencies used to alert rescuers in emergency situations. Each type serves the purpose of aiding in the location and rescue of aircraft personnel in distress.</p></p>
