<p><p>The energy harvesting system market consists of different types, including photovoltaic, thermoelectric, piezo, and electrodynamic. Photovoltaic systems harness solar energy to generate electricity, while thermoelectric systems convert thermal energy into electrical energy. Piezo systems convert mechanical vibrations or pressure into electrical energy, and electrodynamic systems convert kinetic energy into electrical energy using electromagnetic induction. These different types of energy harvesting systems offer various solutions for capturing and converting different forms of renewable energy sources for powering various devices and applications.</p></p>
