<p><p>The air gripper market consists of three main types: friction grip, cradled grip, and encapsulated grippers. Friction grip air grippers utilize a frictional force to securely hold and manipulate objects through the gripping surface. Cradled grip air grippers provide support and hold objects by cradling or wrapping around them. Encapsulated grippers enclose the object within a specialized structure, providing a secure and stable grip. These different types cater to various industrial applications, offering flexibility and reliability in performing gripping tasks.</p></p>
