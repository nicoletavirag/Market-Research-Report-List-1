<p><p>Two-way valve market types can be categorized as full bore or reduced bore. In the full bore market, the valve has a larger opening, allowing for maximum flow through the pipeline. This type of valve is preferred when minimal pressure drop is desired. On the other hand, in the reduced bore market, the valve has a smaller opening, resulting in reduced flow capacity. This type of valve is often used when there are space constraints or when lesser flow rate is sufficient for the process.</p></p>
