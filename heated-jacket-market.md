<p><p>The heated jacket market comprises two main types: electric jackets and battery-operated heated jackets. Electric jackets are powered by a direct electrical connection to a power source, such as a wall outlet or a car battery. They provide a continuous and consistent source of heat, making them suitable for prolonged use. On the other hand, battery-operated heated jackets rely on rechargeable batteries to generate heat. They offer more mobility and convenience as they do not require a constant power supply, making them ideal for outdoor activities or when electricity is not readily available.</p></p>
