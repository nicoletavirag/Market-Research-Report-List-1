<p><p>The skylight window market includes three main types: manual skylights, power skylights, and securing skylights. Manual skylights are operated by hand, typically using a crank or rod, allowing users to open or close the window as desired. Power skylights are automated, utilizing motors or actuators to control the opening and closing of windows through remote or sensor-based systems. Securing skylights, on the other hand, focus on providing enhanced safety and protection, often employing reinforced materials and locking mechanisms to prevent unauthorized access or potential accidents.</p></p>
