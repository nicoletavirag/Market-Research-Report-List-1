<p><p>Eye care devices refer to a wide range of medical devices used for the diagnosis, treatment, and monitoring of various eye-related conditions. The introduction of advanced imaging techniques, such as optical coherence tomography (OCT) and ultrasound biomicroscopy (UBM), has improved diagnosis accuracy and treatment outcomes.
