<p><p>Mechanical circulatory support (MCS) devices are medical technologies used to assist or replace the pumping function of the heart. These devices are categorized into four types based on the area of cardiac support they provide. Left ventricular assist devices (LVADs) support the left side of the heart, right ventricular assist devices (RVADs) support the right side, biventricular assist devices (BiVADs) support both sides simultaneously, and total artificial heart (TAH) devices replace the entire heart. These MCS devices play a crucial role in treating severe heart failure patients by improving blood flow and overall cardiac function.</p></p>
