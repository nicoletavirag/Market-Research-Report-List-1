<p><strong>The Screen Printing Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Mechanism</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Screen printing glass market types can be classified into manual and mechanism markets. In the manual market, screen printing on glass is done manually using handheld squeegees to press ink through a mesh screen onto the glass surface. This type of market is suitable for small-scale and customized printing projects. On the other hand, the mechanism market involves automatic or semi-automatic screen printing machines that use mechanical systems to deposit ink onto glass, allowing for high-speed and large-scale production. Mechanism market is preferred for mass production in industries like automotive, home appliances, and electronics.</p></p>
