<p><p>Metering pumps are used in various industries to accurately measure and control the flow rate of liquids. Diaphragm pumps are a type of metering pump that use a flexible membrane to displace the liquid, ensuring precise and consistent dosing. On the other hand, piston/plunger pumps use a reciprocating piston or plunger to control the liquid flow, offering high-pressure capabilities. Both types of pumps are reliable and efficient, meeting the specific needs of different applications in chemical, water treatment, and other industries.</p></p>
