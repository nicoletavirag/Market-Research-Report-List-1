<p><p>Analog security camera systems are traditional surveillance systems that use analog technology to transmit video signals. Despite the emergence of newer technologies such as IP cameras and network video recorders (NVRs), analog systems continue to hold a significant market share. This is primarily due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. Analog cameras are generally cheaper than their digital counterparts, making them a preferred choice for budget-conscious consumers.</p><p>Moreover, many existing surveillance systems still utilize analog technology, and upgrading to digital systems can be a costly and complex process.
