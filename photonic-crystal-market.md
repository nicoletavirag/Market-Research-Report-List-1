<p><ul><li>One Dimensional Photonic Crystal</li><li>Two Dimensional Photonic Crystal</li><li>Three Dimensional Photonic Crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photonic crystal market can be classified into three types: one-dimensional, two-dimensional, and three-dimensional photonic crystals. One-dimensional photonic crystals are composed of equally spaced layers with alternating refractive indices, allowing them to control the propagation of light in one direction. Two-dimensional photonic crystals consist of a periodic array of rods or holes in a plane, enabling light manipulation in two dimensions. Three-dimensional photonic crystals possess a periodic arrangement of materials in three dimensions, offering precise control of light propagation and more versatile applications.</p></p>
