<p><ul><li>Bath-side Lifts</li><li>Lift in Bathtub</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Bath Lift market consists of two main types: Bath-side Lifts and Lift in Bathtub. Bath-side Lifts are devices that are placed next to the bathtub and can be easily adjusted to lower and raise the user in and out of the tub. Lift in Bathtub, on the other hand, refers to a built-in lift mechanism within the bathtub itself, allowing the user to be lowered into the tub without the need for an additional device. Both types of Electric Bath Lifts provide a safe and convenient way for individuals with mobility issues to enjoy bathing.</p></p>
