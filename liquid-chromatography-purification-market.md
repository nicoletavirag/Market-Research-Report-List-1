<p><p>Liquid chromatography purification is a commonly used technique in the field of analytical chemistry for separating and purifying components of a mixture. It involves passing a sample through a liquid chromatography column, where the components are separated based on their interactions with the stationary phase and mobile phase. The stationary phase is usually a solid material or a liquid immobilized on a solid support, while the mobile phase is a liquid or a gas that carries the sample through the column.</p><p>The liquid chromatography purification market is expected to witness steady growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for advanced analytical techniques in various industries, such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, food and beverage, and environmental testing is a key factor driving market growth.
