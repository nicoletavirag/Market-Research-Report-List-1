<p><p>Product Information Management System (PIMS) market consists of two major types: cloud-based and on-premises systems. A cloud-based PIMS operates on remote servers and allows users to access and manage product information from anywhere with an internet connection, eliminating the need for local hardware and software. On the other hand, an on-premises PIMS is installed and maintained on the user's own servers, providing more control and security but requiring local infrastructure and IT support. Both types offer distinct advantages and cater to different organizational needs in terms of flexibility, scalability, and data security.</p></p>
