<p><p>Wide-range vacuum gauges are used to measure the pressure or vacuum levels in various industrial applications. Indirect measurement gauges are generally more versatile and can measure a wider range of pressures, while direct measurement gauges provide more accurate and precise readings in specific pressure ranges.</p></p>
