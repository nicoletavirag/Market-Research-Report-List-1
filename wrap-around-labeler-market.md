<p><p>Wrap around labelers are used in the packaging industry to apply labels smoothly and accurately onto products or packaging materials. There are two main types of wrap around labelers: automatic and semi-automatic. </p><p>Automatic wrap around labelers are fully automated machines that can handle high-volume labeling tasks. They are equipped with advanced features such as automatic product feeding, label placement, and adhesive application. These labelers are ideal for large-scale production lines where speed and precision are crucial.</p><p>On the other hand, semi-automatic wrap around labelers require some level of manual intervention. Operators load the products onto the machine and initiate the labeling process, but the machine handles the label application. These labelers are suitable for smaller production lines or industries that require flexibility and versatility in labeling operations.</p></p>
