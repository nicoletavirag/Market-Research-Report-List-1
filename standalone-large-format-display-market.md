<p><p>The standalone large format display market comprises three major types: LED-backlit LCD, OLED, and E-Paper. LED-backlit LCD displays use LED lights to illuminate the LCD panel, enabling vibrant colors, high brightness, and energy efficiency. OLED displays utilize organic compounds that emit light when electrically charged, resulting in deep blacks, wide viewing angles, and thin designs. E-Paper displays mimic the appearance of ink on paper, offering low power consumption and excellent readability in various lighting conditions. These displays find applications in digital signage, advertising, gaming, and public information systems.</p></p>
