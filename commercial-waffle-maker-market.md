<p><ul><li>Single-Grid</li><li>Double-Grid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial waffle makers are available in two market types: single-grid and double-grid. The single-grid waffle maker features a single waffle plate, allowing users to cook one waffle at a time. This type is suitable for establishments with lower demand. On the other hand, the double-grid waffle maker has two waffle plates, enabling simultaneous cooking of two waffles. This variation is ideal for high-demand environments, like restaurants or busy breakfast cafes, as it enhances productivity and reduces waiting time for customers.</p></p>
