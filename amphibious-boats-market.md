<p><ul><li>Screw propeller propulsion</li><li>Water jet propulsion</li><li>Track-based propulsion</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Amphibious Boats market involves different types of propulsion methods. Screw propeller propulsion uses a rotating propeller that pushes the boat forward by pushing water backward. Water jet propulsion, on the other hand, employs high-pressure water to propel the boat, providing better maneuverability and shallow water access. Track-based propulsion utilizes tracks instead of wheels, making the boat suitable for various terrains like marshes and swamps. Additionally, there are other types of propulsion methods used in the market, catering to specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
