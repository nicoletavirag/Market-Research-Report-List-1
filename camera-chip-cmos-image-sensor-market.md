<p><strong>The Camera Chip CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Pixel Passive Sensor (PPS)</li><li>Active Pixel Active Sensor (APS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Camera Chip CMOS Image Sensor market is categorized into two types: Passive Pixel Passive Sensor (PPS) and Active Pixel Active Sensor (APS). PPS uses passive sensor technology where each pixel acts as a separate light sensor, providing high resolution but limited sensitivity. On the other hand, APS utilizes active sensor technology with each pixel having its own amplifier, allowing for increased sensitivity and fast data readouts. Both PPS and APS have their unique advantages based on the required application and image quality needs.</p></p>
