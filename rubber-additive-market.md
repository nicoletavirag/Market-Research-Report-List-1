<p><p>Rubber additives are substances that are added to rubber during the manufacturing process to enhance its performance and properties. There are different types of rubber additives available in the market. Antidegradants are additives that help protect rubber from degradation caused by factors such as heat, light, and oxygen. Accelerators are additives that speed up the vulcanization process in rubber production, improving its strength and durability. Other types of rubber additives include plasticizers, fillers, and processing aids, which provide additional properties like flexibility, reinforcement, and ease of processing to the rubber product.</p></p>
