One of the major trends is the adoption of cone-beam computed tomography (CBCT) in dental imaging. <p><strong>The Dental Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extraoral Imaging</li><li>Intraoral Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental imaging market comprises two main types: extraoral imaging and intraoral imaging. Extraoral imaging refers to the imaging of the entire dentition and surrounding structures, such as the jawbone and facial bones. It includes techniques like panoramic radiographs and cone beam computed tomography (CBCT). In contrast, intraoral imaging focuses on capturing detailed images of individual teeth and their supporting structures. This type includes techniques like bitewing radiographs and periapical radiographs. Both types play crucial roles in diagnosing dental conditions and planning treatments.</p></p>
