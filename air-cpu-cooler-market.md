Air CPU coolers are widely used in desktop computers and gaming rigs to prevent the CPU from overheating and ensure optimal performance.</p><p>The future of the air CPU cooler market looks promising. With the increasing adoption of high-performance CPUs and intensive applications like gaming and video editing, the demand for effective cooling solutions is on the rise.
