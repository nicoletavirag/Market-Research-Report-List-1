<p><p>A heat moisture exchanger (HME) is a medical device used to manage the airflow and humidity in the respiratory system of patients who are receiving mechanical ventilation. The HME helps to capture heat and moisture from the patient's exhaled breath and transfers it back to the inhaled air, preventing dehydration and damage to the respiratory system.</p><p>The heat moisture exchanger market is witnessing significant growth due to factors such as the increasing prevalence of respiratory diseases and the rising adoption of mechanical ventilation techniques.
