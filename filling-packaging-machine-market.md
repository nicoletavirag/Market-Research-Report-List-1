<p><strong>The Filling Packaging Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The filling packaging machine market is categorized into two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical filling machines are equipped with a vertical feeding system that enables the automatic filling and sealing of products in upright positions. These machines are commonly used for packaging liquids, pastes, powders, and granules. On the other hand, horizontal filling machines have a horizontal feeding system and are primarily used for filling and sealing solid products in flat positions, such as chocolates, biscuits, bars, and candies. These machines offer different solutions to meet the specific packaging needs of various industries.</p></p>
