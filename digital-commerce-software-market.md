<p><p>Digital commerce software refers to the technology used by businesses to sell products and services online. There are three main types of digital commerce software markets: On-Premise, Cloud-Based, and Hybrid. On-Premise software is installed and managed within a company's own infrastructure, offering full control but requiring maintenance. Cloud-Based software is hosted by a third-party provider, allowing for easy scalability, affordability, and accessibility. Hybrid software combines the advantages of both on-premise and cloud-based solutions, offering flexibility and customization while still leveraging external infrastructure. Each type caters to different business needs and preferences.</p></p>
