<p><ul><li>Single Break Isolator</li><li>Double Break Isolator</li><li>Pantograph type Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC isolators are electrical devices used in power distribution systems to physically disconnect power sources from the circuit. Single break isolators have one set of contacts, while double break isolators have two separate sets of contacts, allowing for enhanced safety. Pantograph type isolators consist of two arms that can be extended or retracted to open or close the circuit. These isolators are widely used in electrical installations to isolate and control power supply from different sources or to isolate faulty components for maintenance and repairs.</p></p>
