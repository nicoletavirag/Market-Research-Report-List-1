<p><p>The commercial solar maintenance market consists of three main types: monitoring, preventative maintenance, and repairs. Preventative maintenance involves regular inspections, cleaning, and testing of the system's components to prevent any potential issues or failures. Repairs refer to the actions taken to fix or replace damaged or malfunctioning components within the solar system.
