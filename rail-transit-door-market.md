<p><ul><li>Exterior Door</li><li>Inner Door</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rail transit door market can be classified into two types: exterior doors and inner doors. Exterior doors refer to the doors located on the exterior of rail transit vehicles, providing access for passengers to enter or exit the vehicle. These doors are designed to be sturdy, weatherproof, and equipped with safety features. Inner doors, on the other hand, are located inside the vehicle and are used to separate different sections or compartments. These doors are typically designed to be lightweight, easy to open and close, and may not have the same level of weatherproofing as exterior doors.</p></p>
