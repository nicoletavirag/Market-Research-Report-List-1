<p><p>Tractor scrapers are heavy equipment used in earthmoving and construction projects. There are two main types of tractor scrapers: wheeled type and crawler type. Wheeled type tractor scrapers are equipped with large wheels, allowing them to move swiftly on flat or slightly uneven surfaces. They are efficient for shorter distances and are commonly used in road construction. On the other hand, crawler type tractor scrapers have tracks similar to tanks, providing better traction and stability on rough terrain. They are ideal for longer distances and heavy-duty tasks in challenging environments.</p></p>
