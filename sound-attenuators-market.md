<p><p>The sound attenuators market can be categorized into several types based on their design and application. Rectangular silencers are commonly used in ventilation and HVAC systems, while elbow silencers are specifically designed to reduce noise at bends and corners of ductworks. Circular silencers are used in round ducts to minimize noise, and other types include splitter silencers, plenum chamber silencers, and acoustic louvers.
