<p><p>Enclosed switches are used in various industries and applications. Panel mount switches are installed directly onto a panel or enclosure, providing a convenient and secure switch option. These switches find applications in industrial equipment, automotive controls, medical devices, and more. Both plunger and panel mount switches offer reliability, durability, and ease of use for efficient operation of electronic systems.</p></p>
