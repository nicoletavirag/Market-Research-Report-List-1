<p><p>Ribbon Fiber Optic Cable market includes two main types: Single-Mode and Multi-Mode. Single-Mode cables are designed for long-distance communication, carrying only a single ray of light through the fiber. They provide higher bandwidth and are used for applications such as telecommunications and data centers. Multi-Mode cables, on the other hand, carry multiple rays of light simultaneously, making them ideal for short-distance transmissions. They are commonly employed in local area networks and indoor installations. These two market segments cater to different needs in terms of speed, distance, and application requirements.</p></p>
