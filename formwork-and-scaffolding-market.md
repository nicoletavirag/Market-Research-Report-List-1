<p><p>Formwork and scaffolding are two important components used in construction projects to support and provide structural stability during the construction process. </p><p>Formwork refers to temporary structures or molds that are used to support and shape poured concrete until it sets and hardens. It is important in ensuring the structural integrity of the final concrete structure and can be made of various materials such as timber, steel, and aluminum. Formwork is commonly used in the construction of buildings, bridges, tunnels, and other concrete structures.</p><p>Scaffolding, on the other hand, is a temporary structure used to provide access to heights and support workers, tools, and materials during construction, maintenance, or repair work. It is typically made of steel or aluminum and consists of platforms, supports, and braces. Scaffolding ensures the safety of workers and allows them to perform tasks at elevated heights.</p><p>The formwork and scaffolding market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 7.3% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to various factors including the increasing investment in infrastructure development projects, especially in emerging economies. Additionally, the growing awareness regarding the benefits of using formwork and scaffolding in construction projects, such as increased efficiency and safety, is driving market growth.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements in formwork and scaffolding systems are also contributing to market growth. The introduction of modular and lightweight systems has made installation and dismantling of formwork and scaffolding easier and quicker. Furthermore, the growing adoption of engineered formwork and scaffolding systems, which offer better adaptability and cost-effectiveness, is fueling market growth.</p><p>Overall, the formwork and scaffolding market is expected to witness continued growth in the coming years, driven by increasing construction activities and the demand for efficient and safe construction processes.</p></p>
