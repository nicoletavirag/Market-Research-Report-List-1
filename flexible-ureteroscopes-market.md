<p><p>Flexible ureteroscopes are medical devices used for diagnosing and treating conditions of the urinary tract, such as kidney stones. There are two types of flexible ureteroscopes: digital ureteroscopes and fiberoptic ureteroscopes. Digital ureteroscopes use advanced imaging technology, such as high-definition cameras and digital processors, to provide clear and detailed images of the urinary tract. Fiberoptic ureteroscopes, on the other hand, use fiber-optic bundles to transmit light and images, providing a direct visualization of the urinary tract. Both types of ureteroscopes are essential tools in urology procedures, offering different benefits and applications.</p></p>
