<p><ul><li>Water Soluble</li><li>Oil Soluble</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Azo polymerization initiators can be categorized into two main types based on their solubility: water soluble and oil soluble. Water soluble initiators are dissolved in water and are commonly used in aqueous polymerization reactions. On the other hand, oil soluble initiators are dissolved in organic solvents and are preferred for polymerization reactions in non-aqueous systems. The choice between water soluble and oil soluble initiators depends on the nature of the polymerization reaction and the desired properties of the final polymer product.</p></p>
