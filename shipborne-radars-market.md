<p><p>Shipborne radars are used for various purposes, including navigation, surveillance, and defense. The market for shipborne radars is classified into three types. First, Passive Electronically Scanned Array (PESA) Radar, which relies on received signals for its operation. Second, Active Electronically Scanned Array (AESA) Radar, which emits its own signals to detect and track targets. Lastly, there are other types of radars, such as frequency modulated continuous wave (FMCW) radar, hybrid radar, and phased array radar. These radars provide enhanced capabilities, improved target detection, and higher operational flexibility for marine vessels.</p></p>
