<p><p>The 3D holographic display market consists of various types, including spinning LED and pseudo holographic display. Spinning LED displays involve the use of LEDs mounted on rotating arms or spindles to create a holographic-like visual effect. Pseudo holographic displays, on the other hand, use a combination of techniques such as reflections, projection, or foils to produce a simulated holographic image. Both types provide immersive visual experiences and are increasingly being utilized for advertising, entertainment, and retail applications.</p></p>
