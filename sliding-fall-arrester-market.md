<p><p>The sliding fall arrester market can be categorized into two main types: non-retractable and retractable. Non-retractable fall arresters are fixed devices that provide continuous protection while working at heights. They are often used in situations where the worker needs to move freely but still requires a secure anchor point. On the other hand, retractable fall arresters offer a more flexible solution. They contain a retractable lifeline that automatically adjusts its length as the worker moves, providing freedom of movement while maintaining safety. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring worker safety at elevated work sites.</p></p>
