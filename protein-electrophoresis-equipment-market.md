<p><p>Protein electrophoresis equipment is a laboratory instrument used to separate proteins based on their size, charge, or both. The equipment consists of a gel matrix, an electric field generator, a power supply, and a detection system. Protein samples are loaded into wells on the gel matrix, and an electric current is applied. The proteins migrate through the gel matrix based on their charge and size, resulting in distinct bands or spots that can be visualized and analyzed.</p><p>The protein electrophoresis equipment market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, such as cancer and cardiovascular diseases, which require protein analysis for diagnosis and treatment, is one of the key factors driving the market growth. Additionally, the rise in research activities in the field of proteomics and personalized medicine is also contributing to the demand for protein electrophoresis equipment.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements in protein electrophoresis equipment, such as improved gel matrices, detection systems, and automation, are further fueling market growth.
