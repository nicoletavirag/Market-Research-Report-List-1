<p><p>Reporter gene assay kits are used for determining the activity of specific genes. The market for these kits is categorized into various types based on the specific assay used. Luciferase assay kits utilize the luciferase enzyme to generate light signals, while fluorescent protein-related assay kits use fluorescent proteins to measure gene expression. Secreted alkaline phosphatase (SEAP) assay kits detect SEAP activity, while chloramphenicol acetyltransferase (CAT) assay kits measure CAT enzyme activity. Beta-galactosidase (LacZ) and beta-glucuronidase (GUS) assay kits detect the activity of these enzymes. Other assay kits encompass various additional methods for detecting gene activity in different research fields.</p></p>
