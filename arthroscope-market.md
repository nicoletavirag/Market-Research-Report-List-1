<p><p>An arthroscope is a type of endoscope used in arthroscopy, a minimally invasive surgical procedure that allows doctors to visualize, diagnose, and treat problems inside a joint. Shoulder arthroscopes are used to examine and treat shoulder joint issues, including rotator cuff tears and shoulder instability.
