<p><ul><li>Shuttle Weaving Machine</li><li>Circular Weaving Machine</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Weaving machines are categorized into various types based on their functioning and mechanism. One type is the shuttle weaving machine, which uses a shuttle to carry the weft yarn across the warp yarns. Another type is the circular weaving machine, which creates fabric in a circular format, often used for producing seamless tubes or bags. The "Others" category encompasses weaving machines that do not fit into the specific shuttle or circular types, including air jet, rapier, and projectile machines. These machines each have their own unique features and applications within the weaving industry.</p></p>
