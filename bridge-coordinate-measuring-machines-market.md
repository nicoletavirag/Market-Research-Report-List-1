<p><p>The bridge coordinate measuring machines market consists of three main types: multi-sensor, optical, and mechanical. Multi-sensor machines utilize various measuring technologies, such as touch probes, lasers, and vision systems, to collect precise dimensional data. Optical machines use non-contact methods, such as lasers or white light scanning, to accurately measure objects. On the other hand, mechanical machines rely on tactile probes to collect data through physical contact with the object. These different types cater to different measurement requirements and considerations, such as the complexity of the object being measured or the desired level of accuracy.</p></p>
