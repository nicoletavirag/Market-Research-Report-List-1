Facial interfaces refer to the masks, nasal pillows, and other accessories used with PAP devices to deliver the air pressure to the patient's airway. Adaptive Servo-Ventilation (ASV) devices provide variable pressure support based on the patient's breathing patterns. The "Others" segment includes alternative therapies, oral devices, and positional therapy aids used to treat sleep apnea.</p></p>
