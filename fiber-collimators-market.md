<p><p>The fiber collimators market finds application in several optical devices such as optical switches, interleavers, couplers, WDMs (Wavelength Division Multiplexers), and others. Optical switches utilize fiber collimators to control the routing of optical signals. Interleavers and couplers employ fiber collimators to separate or combine different optical wavelengths. WDMs utilize collimators to multiplex and demultiplex multiple wavelengths of light in fiber optic communication systems. Other applications of fiber collimators include use in fiber amplifiers, optical sensors, and spectroscopy systems, where they help to efficiently collimate light for various purposes.</p></p>
