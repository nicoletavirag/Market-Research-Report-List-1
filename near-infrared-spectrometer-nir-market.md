<p><p>Near Infrared Spectrometer (NIR) market consists of various types including FT-NIR, UV-Vis-NIR, Raman, and others. FT-NIR spectrometers utilize Fourier transform technology for highly accurate and precise measurements. UV-Vis-NIR spectrometers cover a wide spectral range for versatile applications. Raman spectrometers analyze chemical compositions by measuring scattered light. Other types of NIR spectrometers include dispersive, filter-based, and acousto-optic tunable filter (AOTF) spectrometers, offering different capabilities and functionalities for specific analytical needs in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, and agriculture.</p></p>
