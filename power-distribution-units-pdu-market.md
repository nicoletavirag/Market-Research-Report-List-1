<p><p>Power Distribution Units (PDUs) are devices used in data centers and industrial settings to distribute electrical power to multiple pieces of equipment efficiently and safely. The basic PDU simply provides power outlets without any monitoring or control features. Metering PDUs measure and display power usage information. Monitoring PDUs offer advanced features like remote monitoring and environmental sensors. Switch PDUs have the ability to remotely turn on/off power to individual outlets. Other types of PDUs include intelligent PDUs with advanced analytics capabilities and rack-mount PDUs for easy installation. These different PDU market types cater to diverse power distribution needs.</p></p>
