<p><p>The flexible extraction arm market consists of three main types: wall-mounted, ceiling-mount, and bench-top arms. Wall-mounted arms are attached to the wall and provide flexibility by allowing the arm to be moved in different directions. Ceiling-mount arms are fixed to the ceiling and offer similar flexibility in movement. Bench-top arms are designed to be mounted on a workbench or tabletop, providing efficient extraction in a confined space. These different types cater to different work environments and offer versatile solutions for extracting fumes, dust, and other airborne contaminants.</p></p>
