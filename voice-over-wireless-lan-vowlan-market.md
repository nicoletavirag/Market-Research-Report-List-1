<p><p>Voice over Wireless LAN (VoWLAN) refers to the technology that allows voice communications to be transmitted over a wireless local area network (WLAN). VoWLAN offers increased flexibility and mobility for users, allowing them to communicate effectively within the Wi-Fi network coverage area.</p><p>The Voice over Wireless LAN (VoWLAN) Market is witnessing significant growth due to several factors. First and foremost, the increasing adoption of Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) policies by organizations has led to the demand for VoWLAN solutions, as it allows employees to use their personal devices for work purposes. Additionally, the rising need for cost-effective communication solutions, along with the widespread availability of Wi-Fi networks, is further driving market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the rapid growth of the healthcare sector, where VoWLAN is extensively used for critical communication purposes, is also contributing to market expansion. VoWLAN solutions are employed in hospitals and healthcare facilities to enhance communication between doctors, nurses, and other medical staff. Moreover, the integration of voice communication in applications such as messaging services and unified communication solutions is providing new growth opportunities for the VoWLAN market.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a growing focus on the development of advanced VoWLAN solutions that offer better voice quality and improved security.
