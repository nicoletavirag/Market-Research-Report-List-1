<p><strong>The Circular Dichroism Spectrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linearly Polarized Light Sources</li><li>Circularly Polarized Light Sources</li><li>Multiple Light Sources</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Circular Dichroism Spectrometers are used to measure the difference in absorption of left and right circularly polarized light by chiral molecules. The market for these spectrometers is categorized into three types. Linearly Polarized Light Sources use linearly polarized light to measure the differential absorption. Circularly Polarized Light Sources utilize circularly polarized light to analyze the chiral molecules. Multiple Light Sources employ a combination of linearly and circularly polarized light to provide a more comprehensive analysis of the molecular properties. Each type offers different advantages and can be selected based on specific research requirements.</p></p>
